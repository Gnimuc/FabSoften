17// The 68 facial landmark from the iBUG 300-W dataset(https://ibug.doc.ic.ac.uk/resources/facial-point-annotations/):
18// Jaw: 0-16 (lower face boundary)
19// Right eyebrow: 17-21
20// Left eyebrow: 22-26
21// Nose: 27-35
22// Right eye: 36-41
23// Left eye: 42-47
24// Mouth: 48-59 (inner boundary: 60-67)
25// Right cheek: 1 2 3 4 31 32 33 30 29 28 39 40 41 36 1
26// Left cheek: 15 14 13 12 35 34 33 30 29 28 42 47 46 45 15
